"Young fashion designer"

for children and teenagers from 8 to 16 years old

About the course

After several lessons in fashion design, each student creates something new and interesting. The goal of our teachers is not to make someone repeat their findings, but to show that the world of imagination is limitless. Young designers must forget the words "difficult" and "impossible" - and then they will achieve anything they want.
